# Personality & Thought Tools

## Personality tools
- Enneagram of Personality (see linked resources).

## Troubleshooting quick checks
- Depressed/anxious: focus on present.
- Headache: drink water.
- Weight: reduce sugar; consider safe fasting with medical advice.
- Feet: roll a golf ball; orthotics.
- Lack of motivation: cold shower.
- Paranoid: try pronoia.
- Sounds: ASMR/desensitization.
- Stressed: declutter.
- Rage: scream at trees or write.
- Too many side projects: monotask; prioritize lists.

## Relationship tools
- Acceptance; apologize; give people what they want; avoid coercion.
- Couples therapy; "let’s disconnect" protocol; shared lists; active listening; safe spaces; boundary setting.

## Performance & ego tools
- HALT (Hungry, Angry, Lonely, Tired) strategies; hydration; gratitude; mnemonics; mind palaces; mentorship; journaling.

## Practical aids
- Ergonomics, sleep aids (talk to clinician), vitamins, totems, volunteering.
