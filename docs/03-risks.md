# Negative Traits, Risks & Medical Correlations

## Negative traits / things to watch
- Anxiety, depression, existential dread.
- Codependence, coercion, manipulation — remove coercion from relationships.
- Paranoia, conspiratorial thinking (seek metaphorical lessons).
- Crime temptation (do not act on it).
- Embarrassment, employer exploitation, hoarding, isolation.
- Judgmental tendencies, low self-esteem, mania and crashes.
- Reframing lies as jokes, object impermanence, doom-piling, OCD traits.
- Projection, provocation, psychosis, rage, retaliation, spiraling.
- Stalking, idea theft, stonewalling, substance abuse, suicide risk.
- Synesthesia and strong emotional-physical reactions.

## Spookiest things
- Delusions and false pattern-finding.
- Schizophrenia: keep detailed notes, compare external facts, isolate variables.
- Psychosis: drug-induced, trauma-related, or primary — seek clinical help.
- Self-diagnosis biases can mislead assessment.

## Medical correlations (summary)
- Autism associated with higher risk for cardiovascular disease and metabolic issues; research varies — consult primary literature.
- Some studies suggest modestly increased overall cancer risk in ASD populations; evidence mixed.
